WHAT IT DOES
Slackbooks is a helpful slackbot that can help small and medium sized businesses track expenses through Quickbooks Online, powered by Intuit. With slackbooks, teams can work together through Slack, which is already a popular system for team discussions and communications, and update, delete, or write expense entries into Quickbooks Online.




Chat

Everyone’s favourite slackbot just got smarter and more interactive

Collaborate

Work with your team members directly in slack to directly interact with Quickbooks Online

Expense

Send Slackbot commands to input, delete, and edit expenses directly to Quickbooks Online


HOW IT WORKS
We enabled the slackbooks bot to directly interact with Quickbooks Online and help team members collaborate with updating transactions.
